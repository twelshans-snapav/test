Customer hand-off and payment

Follow these instructions to verify the Customer's information in OvrC before handing the system off to the customer through the Control4 mobile app.

Verifying and editing Customer information before hand-off

Follow the steps below to verify the customer’s information is accurate in OvrC.

  1. Click List on the left-side menu.

  2. Click the customer.

  3. Review the customer’s information for accuracy.

    • If the customer’s physical address is inaccurate, click the three dots in the upper, right-hand corner of the page and click Edit Location.

  • If the customer’s name, phone number, or email address is inaccurate, click the three dots in the upper, right-hand corner of the page and click Edit Customer.

  1. Make your changes and click Save.

Handing off the Project to the Customer

The following steps detail the customer hand-off process that an integrator completes when ready to provide the customer access to their OvrC account.

To get started, follow the steps below to invite the customer to their project via OvrC.

  1. Click List from the left-side menu and click the customer.

  2. Click the Settings tab.

  3. Click Invite User.

  1. Enter the customer’s email address in the Email Address field.

  1. Select the Control4 and OvrC Connect checkboxes.

  1. Click SEND INVITE.

The customer receives an email from Control4 with a one-time use passcode to log into their OvrC account. The invitation expires after five days. Afterward a new invite must be generated.

Customer actions

The remainder of the customer hand-off process is completed within the Control4 app. Ensure the customer completes the following steps to successfully log into their OvrC account.

  1. The customer downloads the Control4 app from Apple App Store or Google Play Store and clicks on Register Your System.

  1. The customer reviews and agrees to the Control4 Terms & Privacy Policy.

  1. The customer creates their account by entering the one-time use passcode, their email address, and a new password. 

  1. The customer reviews, confirms, or edits their information.

  1. The customer reviews information about the required Connect annual plan.

    CA-1 projects

    Free

    Single Core Lite project

    $99 plus tax, yearly

    All other scenarios

    $249 plus tax, yearly

Note: -__.If the customer upgrades to a new controller, a new Connect payment plan is sent to the customer. Meaning, if a CA1 project adds one Core Lite to the project, they are immediately upgraded to a $99 yearly plan. If the customer upgrades to multiple Core Lites or a CORE 1 controller and above, they are upgraded to the $249 plan.

  1. The customer views additional support options for Assist and Assist Premium plans. Click or tap Compare Plans to see the features available for each. Then select Assist, Assist, Premium, or Decline.

    Assist is an additional $899 per year. Assist Premium is an additional $2,999 per year.

  1. The customer reviews their selected plan summary.

Note: -__.The total cost includes the price of Connect and the selected support option.

  1. The customer selects their preferred method of payment and enters their payment information.

  1. The customer completes the registration process and taps or clicks Learn Control4.

  1. The customer is brought to the main screen of their mobile app experience.
