Request Features and Report Bugs

You can help shape OvrC by giving us feedback—in fact, we welcome it!

Suggestions, ideas, and bug reports from installers like you have already shaped the OvrC platform. Help us make OvrC even more useful for you!

Access the feedback tool

Log in to app.OvrC.com. Click the Support link at the top right. The link won't appear if your browser window is too small; in that case, click your profile image to make a dropdown appear.

Check to see if the issue has already been requested

The Changelog window shows feedback posts that have already been submitted.

In the upper right, use the search tool to look for keywords related to your request. Click any post's title to see the full text.

If you find that someone else has already submitted your request, click this arrow to give the topic an upvote and you're done!

You can also leave a comment if you have additional information.

Create a new request

If you can't find your request in the system, add it yourself.

In the dialog to the left, type a title for your post in the text box provided. Make sure it is descriptive and easy to search ("Daylight Saving Doesn't Work" as opposed to "Bug"

Type a complete description of the request in the text box given. Be complete, but don't be too wordy.

At your option, you can add a photo. If you are reporting a bug and have a screenshot, or if you have a diagram of the feature you want, that can be worth a thousand words. Click the Image icon at the lower left to add a graphic file. Select the image file, and click Open.

Click Create Post, and OvrC adds your post to the list for everyone to review.

Follow up

You can check the progress of your request whenever you access the feedback window.

Click the filter dropdown and select My Own to see the status of all posts you've submitted, including the upvotes and comments received.

You'll find our staff frequently interacts with requests, and it’s very helpful if you answer any questions they have.

Better yet, if your post has a status of Under Review, Planned, In Progress, or Complete, that means your request is being developed our programmers, and you have made OvrC a better service for your needs!
