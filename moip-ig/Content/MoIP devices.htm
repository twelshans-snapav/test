MoIP devices

MoIP Controller (B-900-MOIP-4K-CTRL)

  1. Status LED - Solid during the startup process. Blinks during normal operation.

  2. Reboot button - Use a pin to press the recessed button to restart the controller.

  3. USB ports - For use with a USB-to-Ethernet adapter. See the Network Troubleshooting section for more information.

  4. Network/LAN port - Connect to the MoIP switch to provide access to MoIP transmitters and receivers.

  5. HDMI port - Not used for the MoIP system.

  6. 12VDC power connection - Connect the provided power supply.

  7. Factory Default button - Use a pin to press the recessed button 3 times within 10 seconds to restore factory settings.

MoIP Video Transmitter (B-900-MOIP-4K-TX)

  1. Power indicator - On: Powered on | Blinking: Starting up | Off: Powered off.

  2. System status light - On: Connected to network and detecting a source | Off: Does not detect a network connection| Blinking: Detects a network connection but not a source.

  3. Network port - Provides access to the network and Power-over-Ethernet (PoE).

  4. DIP switches - Switch 1: IR receiver on/off | Switch 2: DTE/DCE.

  5. 3.5 mm Input - Analog audio embedding to replace HDMI audio.

  6. L/R Audio Out - RCA analog audio de-embedding of 2-channel PCM audio.

  7. RS-232 - RJ45, RS-232, (TX, RX, Ground), EIA-561 pinout.

  8. HDMI In/Out - HDMI source input and HDMI loop output for local display.

  9. IR Receiver - 3.5 mm mini mono/stereo for external receivers.

  10. IR Flasher - 3.5 mm mini mono output to IR emitter.

  11. Factory Reset - Press and hold for 10 seconds to restore to factory settings.

  12. 12V 2A power connection - Optional 12VDC 2A locking connection for external power supply (not included).
